Where'd I get this?

Before I dig into the guts and glory of this machine, let's put some perspective and expectations on what this box is to me.

It's a curiosity.

I picked this machine up off the "Free Table", if I recall, at the WoRC in 2022. It'd been sitting on my shelf in my back room since the day before I started writing this. I had no expectations on what this thing is, what it'd do. It was, and kind of still is, a mystery to me, in what its purpose is. Wikipedia has a short description on it's intent, but, doesn't exactly describe what its purpose or use for the end user was meant to be.

I suppose that at the end of the day it is a device that allows people from developing countries to get online. This is not a work horse, by any stretech of the imagination.
